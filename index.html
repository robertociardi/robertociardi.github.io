About Me

I've grown with LEGO, computers and family. I'm a fond of science and technology and I'd like to know and discover everything: everything about world, everything about history, everything about us, everything about me.
It's pretty difficult, so I've decided to choose the "easy way" and I've become a Computer Engineer at University of Pisa. Then I've realized I was not too cool and my work were preatty easy to understand, so I've decided to study Embedded Computing System: the longer the name, the cooler the topic!
Now, I'm actually finishing my way through different exams: I've studied Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Innovation, Embedded sys... but I'm not stopping here.... I'm an ironic boy, in the body of a man, who enjoy things as a child.
